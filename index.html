Welcome To Iowa Avian Resources

Welcome to the future of genetically pure captive-bred raptors.

In selecting our foundation stock, we took the time and put forth the effort to aquire the most exceptional representations of each species. This has allowed us to propogate the world's finest captive-bred hunting birds.

We are home to the largest genetically pure Finnish Goshawk breeding population as well as housing the only breeding population of Siberian Goshawks found on the North American continent.

Whether you are looking for a Gyrfalcon, Peales Peregrine, Gyr/Peregrine Hybrid, or a European Sparrowhawk, we can supply you with superior genetics for your falconry or propagation needs.

Our waitlists fill up quickly, so please contact us early to secure a spot!
